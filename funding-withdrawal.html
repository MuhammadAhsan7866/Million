The Benefits of the Mx Market Wallet

Instant deposits and withdrawals

Make deposits to your wallet immediately upon registering to become a live trader to ensure your funds are ready to trade with as soon as your account is approved. The wallet also allows you to receive instant withdrawals from your trading account, whenever you choose to withdraw. 

Risk Management

The MX MARKETS Wallet provides a secure hub for you to store your funds in, away from unpredictable and volatile market conditions. You can also use it to transfer funds only as and when you are willing to risk them, while keeping your capital securely separate from your trading account the rest of the time.

One Wallet for Each Currency

You can choose to split your capital further by opening one MX MARKETS wallet in each available currency: PLN, USD, GBP & EUR.

Fast-Track the KYC Process

Newly registered accounts with funded Mx Market Wallets will be given priority by our KYC team when conducting the document approval process. Fund your wallet upon opening your account to ensure you start trading as soon as possible!
