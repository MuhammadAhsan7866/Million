Why Traders Choose the NDD Model

Precise execution

Effortless, quick, and stress-free With no dealing desk, we can guarantee that trades are executed promptly and accurately without interruptions or broker interference. In particular under erratic market conditions, this results in a smoother trading experience.

There is no trading desk arrangement that guarantees direct access to the most extensive liquidity. Due to this, MX Markets can provide clients with premium trading conditions and access to tier-1 institutions.

Transparency

The NDD mechanism encourages communication between traders and brokers. Full disclosure of the execution model serves as a litmus test for the company's ethical business practices and the client experience with it.
