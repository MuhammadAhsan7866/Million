About the Forex Market

The foreign exchange (FX) market is the biggest and busiest financial online market in the world. You trade the value of one currency against another in this market.

Customers at MX Markets can trade foreign exchange pairs using Contracts for Difference (CFDs). You can make money off of changes in currency rates by using CFD FX trading. You achieve this by taking a trading position based on your investigation and evaluation of the possible value movement of a currency.

Using this strategy, you can, depending on your price estimates, take either a purchase (long) or sell (short) position on a currency.
