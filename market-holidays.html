Risk Warning: Trading foreign exchange on margin involves a significant risk and may not be suitable for all investors. Prior to engaging in forex trading, it's important to thoroughly assess your investment goals, experience level, and risk tolerance. There is a chance that you could incur a loss of part or all of your investment, so it's crucial not to invest funds you can't afford to lose. Familiarize yourself with all the potential risks linked to forex trading and consider seeking guidance from an independent financial advisor if uncertainty arises. Please note that MX Markets does not provide its services to residents of specific jurisdictions, including Mauritius and North Korea.
